When searching or analyzing products on Temu:

1. **Search Endpoint Structure**:
   - `https://www.temu.com/search_result.html?search_key=QUERY`

2. **Sorting & Filter Parameters**:
   - Price (Low to High): `&sort_type=price_asc`
   - Top Selling / Best Match: `&sort_type=sales`
   - Customer Reviews: `&sort_type=rating`

3. **Product Information Extraction**:
   - Extract product title, current sale price, original list price, discount percentage, and customer ratings/orders count.
   - Provide direct clickable Markdown links to the Temu item page.
   - Group products by price range or discount percentage in a summary table.
